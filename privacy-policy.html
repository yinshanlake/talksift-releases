Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

TalkSift ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

  • Audio data: Voice input is processed in real-time to generate text. Audio is not stored after processing is complete.
  • Generated text: The distilled text output is stored locally on your device. We do not retain copies on our servers.
  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication and communication.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., feature usage frequency) to improve the product. No personal content is included.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Process your voice input and return distilled text
  • Send you product updates and waitlist notifications (with your consent)
  • Improve our AI models using aggregated, anonymized patterns — never your personal content
  • Maintain and improve app performance

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do not store your audio recordings
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your personal content to train AI models
  • We do not share identifiable data with advertisers

4. Data Security

All data transmission between your device and our servers uses TLS encryption. Audio processing occurs in isolated, ephemeral environments that are destroyed after each session.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud processing (speech-to-text and AI distillation)
  • Analytics (anonymized usage data only)
  • Email delivery (for waitlist and product updates)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality (e.g., session management). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

TalkSift is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@talksift.app.
