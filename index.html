Installation Guide

How to install TalkSift on macOS

Since TalkSift isn't on the App Store yet, macOS may show a security warning. Here's how to open it — takes 10 seconds.

1

Open the DMG file

Double-click the downloaded TalkSift.dmg and drag TalkSift to your Applications folder.

2

Try to open TalkSift

Double-click TalkSift in your Applications folder. macOS will show a warning — this is normal. Click "Done" to close it. (Don't worry, we'll fix this in the next step.)

3

Open System Settings

Click the Apple menu in the top-left corner of your screen, then click "System Settings".
